Roles in project:

Blackwater Haven

Implemented a sailing mechanic where sail orientation relative to wind direction affects boat velocity.
Players can tack against the wind by zig-zagging similar to real sailing.

Thief Game

A game where the player goal is to steal a soda recipe. The player have to avoid cameras,
find door codes to find his way to the recepie. The player can drink soda to sprint for a short while
